What's cooking

From the kitchen

A short, honest menu of Mexican favourites — built around fresh ingredients and salsas made in-house. Here's a taste of what we serve.

Street Tacos

Soft corn tortillas, slow-cooked fillings, fresh coriander, onion and a squeeze of lime — the way they're meant to be.

Loaded Nachos

Crisp corn chips piled with cheese, beans, jalapeños and our pico de gallo — a proper sharing plate for the table.

Burritos & Bowls

Generously filled burritos and burrito bowls with rice, beans, your choice of filling and toppings to build it your way.

House Salsas & Sides

Guacamole, pico de gallo and salsas made fresh in-house, plus the sides that round out the plate.

Drinks & Aguas

Mexican sodas, aguas frescas and cold drinks to go with the food — something for every age at the table.

Catering & Functions

Feeding a crowd? Talk to us about platters and catering for birthdays, work do's and gatherings around Darfield.

Our story

A taste of Mexico, here in Darfield

La Mexicana brings real Mexican flavours to the Darfield main street — the kind of food you want to share. Everything is made fresh, from the salsas to the fillings.

Whether you're after a quick taco on the way through or settling in for dinner with the family, you'll get a friendly welcome and a plate worth coming back for.
