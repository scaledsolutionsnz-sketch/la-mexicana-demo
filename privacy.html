Privacy Policy

How La Mexicana collects, uses and looks after the information you share with us.

This is placeholder content for the demo and should be reviewed before going live.

1. Information we collect

When you contact us through this website, we may collect the details you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number and the content of your message or feedback.

2. How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, manage bookings and catering, and improve our food and service. We do not sell your information.

  • To reply to your enquiry or booking request
  • To arrange catering or takeaway orders
  • To understand and act on your feedback

3. Form submissions

Our website forms are processed by a third-party form service (FormSubmit) which delivers your message to our email inbox. Your details are handled in line with that service's own privacy practices.

4. Keeping your information safe

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share and keep it only as long as needed to respond to you and meet our obligations.

5. Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by getting in touch using the details below.
